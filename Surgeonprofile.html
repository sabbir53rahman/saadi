Dr Metin Küçükkaya

Dr Küçükkaya is a globally renowned orthopaedic surgeon, boasting a highly distinguished career spanning over three decades. He is a pioneer of novel surgical methods for cable bone transport and retrograde intramedullary nail extension and an expert in classical Ilizarov and internal osteosynthesis surgical techniques. Dr Küçükkaya was the first surgeon in the world to implant the Nuvasive PRECICE™ Magnetic Extension nail system and, in 2019, the Synoste smart memory alloy nail system. He currently consults for leading orthopaedic technology innovators Globus Medical Inc.

A world-leading researcher in the field of orthopaedic medicine, Dr Küçükkaya is a member of a large number of learned organisations, including the Turkish Association of Orthopaedics and Traumatology, AO Trauma, and The Limb Lengthening and Reconstruction Society: ASAMI–North America. He co-directs the globally-renowned annual International 3D Surgery Course, Munich, which offers clinicians training and education on the latest innovations in 3D surgery, surgical techniques and implant technology.

With a zero percent long-term post-surgical complication rate, Dr Küçükkaya is a meticulous surgeon dedicated to realising the best possible outcomes for those under his care, and particularly for those patients who have suffered complications under the care of other surgeons.
