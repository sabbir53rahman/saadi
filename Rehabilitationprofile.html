Kinemed

Istanbul’s Kinemed Rehabilitation Clinic, located in the Nişantaşı Quarter of Istanbul is run by brother and sister team, Arman and Mari Apelyan (MSc PT). Considered to be among Turkey’s most qualified physiotherapists, Arman and Mari studied at the prestigious Rubin Institute for Advanced Orthopaedics in Baltimore, Maryland under renowned specialists Drs Dror Paley and Anil Bhave.

Building their practice around the very latest innovations in the field of physiotherapeutic medicine, Kinemed offers remote and in-person, singular and combination treatment options to improve your wellbeing and support your recovery, including a broad range of manipulation and mobilisation therapies, advanced techniques for limb lengthening rehabilitation and deformity correction, massage, acupuncture, pilates, heat therapy, cold therapy, gait analysis, neurokinetic therapy, proprioceptive neuromuscular facilitation, kinesio-taping, extracorporeal shockwave therapy (ESWT), C, and dry-needling. Contracture management, Functional Electrical Stimulation (FES), and gait re-education.
