Asude

Opened in January 2023, the 60-room Asude Health Facility is located in the heart of Istanbul’s affluent Şişli district, and close to the popular cafes, exclusive stores, chic boutiques, salons and restaurants of the stunning Nişantaşı Quarter.

Rooms boast state-of-the-art antibacterial air conditioning systems, Smart TVs (including Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, YouTube, Google and Twitch), Wi-Fi, private en-suite and personal tea and coffee making facilities. Guests can also enjoy an array of hot and cold drinks, local cakes and snacks from our in-house café, relax in our shared social spaces, or spend time enjoying views across the city from one of two outdoor terraces.

Asude offers a range of facilities to support recovery, including orthopaedic beds and a bright, spacious and well-equipped on-site rehabilitation centre, including parallel bars, exercise bikes, elliptical (cross) trainers, physiotherapy stairs and step machines, yoga balls and a range of resistance equipment.

Delicious, nutritious meals curated by our own registered dietician are prepared by our qualified chefs using fresh, organic, seasonal ingredients sustainably grown at Asude’s own private farm. Create a bespoke, customisable diet plan incorporating a range of tantalising Turkish and international meat-based, vegetarian and vegan dishes, or request a familiar favourite and our staff will be happy to prepare it for you. Special dietary requirements? Our inclusive menu can accommodate all palettes, preferences and dietary restrictions.

Alongside our friendly on-site reception staff, the Asude team include trained physicians, neurologists, physical therapists and nutritionists, as well as a multi-lingual support staff available 24 hours a day to provide discrete, professional, world-class care built around your individual requirements.

If you need to keep an eye on business during your time at Asude, why not take advantage of our 24hr private office space? With business grade amenities including high-speed broadband (hard-wired ethernet and secure Wi-Fi), collaborative phone and teleconferencing facilities and business essentials including printer, fax and document shredder, you can be certain that you won’t miss a step, however far away from the office you may be.
