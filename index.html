Who we are

Everybody deserves the opportunity to live an active, healthy life, and here at IOG we’re working hard to ensure that everybody can. Providing knowledge, understanding and resources around orthopaedic care, we exist to provide what others can’t.

Whether you need support with a sports injury, an elective choice, or a chronic or congenital condition, whether you have faced barriers to care, or whether you have suffered complications in the past, our world-class orthopaedic surgeons, care-givers and rehabilitation specialists will work with you to design a personalised treatment plan and empower you to realise a better quality of life.

We are committed to the delivery of person-centred care excellence at every stage of the journey, for everybody.
